About KanTrack

KanTrack is a privacy-first, local-only Kanban board built for focus and clarity. No accounts. No cloud. No distractions. Just you and your work.

Features

  • Four-column board - To Do, In Progress, On Hold, Done
  • Rich task details with notes, timers, priorities, due dates & tags
  • Built-in Notebook for freeform notes and documentation
  • World clocks and chronometer widgets
  • Full-text search, column filters, and tag filters
  • Keyboard shortcuts for efficient navigation
  • Export to PDF, HTML, JSON, and encrypted backup
  • Undo / redo for all task actions

Where Your Data Lives

Tasks, notes, tags, history localStorage + IndexedDB -> your browser only
Notebook pages & images IndexedDB -> your browser only
UI preferences localStorage -> your browser only
Encryption keys Never stored -> derived per-session from your password

Nothing is sent to any server. KanTrack has no backend, no accounts, and makes no network requests. All storage is local to your browser.

What KanTrack Protects Against

  • XSS via imported data - an allowlist-based sanitizer strips all event handlers and unsafe tags before any HTML is rendered to the page
  • Inline script injection - a strict Content Security Policy (script-src 'self') blocks inline scripts and javascript: URL execution
  • Malicious import files - the sanitizer also runs on content from imported .kantrack.json workspace files
  • Encrypted exports use AES-256-GCM with PBKDF2 key derivation (100,000 iterations, SHA-256)

Known Limitations - Read These

  • Device compromise - if your device is infected or physically accessed, data in localStorage and IndexedDB is readable. KanTrack cannot protect against OS-level access.
  • Browser extensions - extensions with broad permissions can read page content and storage. Use a minimal-extension browser profile for sensitive data.
  • Unencrypted exports - standard .kantrack.json exports are plain JSON. Use the encrypted export for sensitive data.
  • Data at rest - KanTrack does not encrypt data in localStorage or IndexedDB. Anyone with access to your logged-in account can read it via browser DevTools.
  • Password strength - the encrypted export is only as strong as the password you choose.

We cannot protect your data if your device is compromised. That is an honest limitation we cannot engineer away.

Support KanTrack ☕

KanTrack is free and always will be! No ads, no accounts required, no tracking. We built it this way on purpose, because we believe your workspace is yours alone.

Why your support matters

Keeping KanTrack alive has real costs, the domain, the infrastructure, and the time it takes to build things right. There are no investors or corporate backing behind this. It's just us, building something we believe in. If KanTrack helps you stay organized and clear-headed, a coffee goes a long way.

What we're building next

Your support helps fund the next chapter of KanTrack:

  • Encrypted Cloud Vault - zero-knowledge backup. The server will never be able to read your data, only you can.
  • Multi-device Sync - your board, seamlessly current across all your devices, with conflict resolution that stays out of your way.

All of this is optional. Local Mode stays free and fully featured, forever. No paywalls on existing features, ever.
