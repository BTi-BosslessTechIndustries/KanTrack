# KanTrack Manifesto
## Personal Workflow. Personal Control. Personal Clarity.

KanTrack exists for one reason:

> To give individuals a private, clear, and controlled way to organize their work.

In a world where productivity tools increasingly serve organizations, metrics, and oversight, KanTrack serves the person doing the work.

---

## Why KanTrack Exists

Modern work is fragmented across systems designed for coordination and visibility, not for personal clarity.

KanTrack restores a simple capability:

👉 Knowing what matters right now.

It is not a management tool.  
It is not a reporting system.  
It is not a performance tracker.  

It is a personal command center for navigating complex work.

---

## What KanTrack Believes

### Work should not require surrendering control of your workflow.
Your plan belongs to you.

### Clarity is more valuable than features.
Seeing clearly beats doing more.

### Privacy is a default, not an option.
Your work is not data to be harvested.

### Independence matters.
The tool should work on your terms, not the provider’s.

### Trust is earned by restraint.
An app should not become a trap.

---

## What KanTrack Is

A focused personal work platform centered on the kanban board as a thinking surface.

A place to:

- plan your day  
- track your responsibilities  
- organize context and notes  
- regain control over fragmented tasks  

It complements organizational tools without replacing them.

If Jira tracks projects, KanTrack tracks your day.

---

## What KanTrack Is Not

KanTrack will never become:

- a surveillance tool  
- a productivity scoring system  
- a management dashboard  
- a gamified task app  
- a platform designed for oversight  

If a feature exists primarily to observe the user rather than serve them, it does not belong.

---

## The Design Philosophy

KanTrack should feel like:

- a calm workspace  
- a clear desk  
- a private planning surface  

Not a corporate dashboard.

The goal is a single emotional outcome:

👉 Feeling organized and in control.

---

## The Long View

KanTrack aims to become:

### The Personal Operating System for Work

A trusted layer that helps individuals navigate professional life without losing autonomy.

Growth will not come from feature expansion, but from alignment with this purpose.

---

## Final Principle

KanTrack is built on a simple idea:

> Your work is yours. Your plan should be too.

---

## Repository Purpose

This repository contains the philosophy, principles, and documents that guide KanTrack’s development.

They exist to ensure the product remains aligned with its purpose regardless of scale, success, or pressure.

---

**KanTrack is an app — not a trap.**
