# Privacy Policy

## Overview

KanTrack is a local-first personal workflow tool.

By default, all data is stored locally on the user's device, and no network connection is required.

Optional cloud features may be enabled by users who choose to create an account.

---

## Local Mode (Default)

In local mode:

- Data is stored entirely on the user's device
- No account is required
- No data is transmitted externally
- No tracking or analytics occur

---

## Optional Cloud Mode

If the user enables an account:

- Network connectivity is used for backup and synchronization
- Only the user's KanTrack data is transmitted
- No behavioral analytics or tracking are collected

Cloud features are strictly opt-in.

---

## Data Collection

KanTrack does not collect:

- Personal information (unless voluntarily provided for account use)
- Usage analytics
- Tracking data
- Behavioral metrics

---

## Cookies and Storage

Local browser storage is used only to persist user data on the device.

Tracking cookies are not used.

---

## Data Ownership

Users retain full control of their data and may export or delete it at any time.

---

## Security Responsibility

Users are responsible for securing their devices and managing backups of local data.

---

## Source Transparency

KanTrack is source-available so users can verify how data is handled.

---

## Philosophy

Offline by default. Online by choice.
